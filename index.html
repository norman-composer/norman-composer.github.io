About Norman Schmidt

Norman Schmidt is a guitarist and composer in the genre of Film-/Game-Soundtrack and Metal

He composed Music for the Movie Home - Zu Hause bist du tot by Michelle Kevenhörster and for the Indie-Game Good Night Annabella of a developer-group of the Westphalian University Of Applied Sciences

He also composes Music for his 3 Chapter Solo-Album.
